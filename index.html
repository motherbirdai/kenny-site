Philosophy
01
Compress complexity

Technology should make powerful things simple and keep them honest. If it creates more noise than clarity, it's broken.

02
Ship, don't pitch

Ideas are cheap. Deployed products are the only proof that matters. Build, iterate, let users decide.

03
Sovereignty by default

From crypto to AI, the thread is the same: give people control over their own data, tools, and decisions. Eight years of building on that principle.
